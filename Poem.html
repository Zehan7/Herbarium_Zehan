Poem

A love letter to earth

Oh wondrous blue, our cherished home, With skies that stretch, with seas that roam. Mountains rise, their peaks so high, A canvas kissed by painted sky. The forests hum with whispered song, As creatures dance the whole night long. The rivers weave a silver thread, Through valleys lush, where life is spread. Yet heed the call, her voice is clear, She pleads for us, year by year. Protect the trees, the air, the ground, A harmony so vast, profound. So on this day, renew the vow, To cherish Earth, from root to bough. For all she gives, let love repay, And honor her this Earth Day.
